How to Use the Worksheets

Teachers can print out the worksheets from WonderTeach for free, and use them in their classrooms to help students learn.

FAQ

What grade level are the worksheets appropriate for? The worksheets are appropriate for grades 1-5.

How often are new worksheets added to WonderTeach? As I am the only one making the worksheets, it is difficult to upload many worksheets daily. However, I aim to upload atleast 1-2 worksheets every business week for each grade level. This means, I upload 1-2 worksheets for Grade 1 on Monday, 1-2 worksheets for Grade 2 on Tuesday, and so on.

Is there a limit to how many worksheets I can download or print? No, there is no limit to how many worksheets you can download or print. All of the worksheets are free to download and print without a subscription.

Are there answer keys available for the worksheets? Yes, answer keys are available for all worksheets, and they are free to download and print.

Are the worksheets designed to align with any particular curriculum or standard? The worksheets are designed with Common Core standards in mind.
